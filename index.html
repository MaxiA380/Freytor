Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to set up Freytor?

Most teams are up and running within 15 minutes. Simply connect your carrier accounts, invite your team, and you're ready to go. No technical expertise required.

Which carriers do you support?

We support 100+ carriers globally including DHL, FedEx, UPS, USPS, and many regional carriers in emerging markets. If you don't see your carrier, let us know and we'll add it.

Can I try Freytor before committing?

Yes! We offer a 30-day free pilot (not just a trial) with full access to all features. No credit card required. We’ll even import your first 20 shipments for free and show you the savings during a guided onboarding call.

Do I need GPS hardware or special devices?

Not at all. Freytor is 100% hardware-free. We use carrier APIs and simple driver check-ins through our mobile app for full visibility — no GPS trackers, no expensive devices, no installation required.

What makes Freytor different from enterprise logistics tools?

Freytor gives you enterprise capabilities — AI delay predictions, CVRP route optimization, proof of delivery, driver app, fleet management, and sustainability routing — all at SME-friendly pricing. No contracts, no hardware, setup in under 10 minutes.

Is my data secure with Freytor?

Your data is encrypted in transit (256-bit SSL) and at rest. We perform daily encrypted backups, enforce strict access controls, and never share your data with third parties. SOC 2 Type II certification is currently in progress. We follow GDPR principles — you can request deletion of your data at any time via privacy@freytor.com.
