Proof of Delivery lets your drivers upload photos, digital signatures, and delivery notes directly from their phone when a shipment is completed. It automatically closes the shipment record and notifies the customer. This eliminates delivery disputes, provides accountability, and gives you a digital paper trail. Available on Growth and Enterprise plans.

CVRP (Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem) optimization considers vehicle weight and volume limits when planning multi-stop routes for multiple drivers. Unlike basic routing, CVRP ensures no vehicle is overloaded and finds the most fuel-efficient paths. Our Growth plan includes CVRP with capacity and multi-stop support, while Enterprise adds a full Google OR-Tools solver integration and EV/sustainability routing. Users typically see 10-20% fuel savings.
