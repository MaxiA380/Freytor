Our Journey

Born from frustration, built with purpose

Freytor started when our founders witnessed firsthand the daily struggles of small business owners trying to manage logistics. They watched friends juggle 5+ carrier portals, battle with broken spreadsheets, and coordinate deliveries through chaotic WhatsApp groups.

The enterprise solutions existed, but they were designed for Fortune 500 companies with Fortune 500 budgets. Where was the solution for the bakery shipping nationwide? The e-commerce startup with 50 daily orders? The distributor serving regional stores?

That's when we decided: if the right solution doesn't exist, we'll build it ourselves.
